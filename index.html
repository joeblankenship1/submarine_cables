Submarine Cable Map



This map shows the international active and planned submarine cable systems and their landing stations as collected and cataloged by TeleGeography's Global Bandwidth research.

The map shows cables with a maximum capacity of approximately 5 Gbps. The locations of the cables and landing points on the map does not reflect the actual infrastructure locations.
